== Description ==

Add YouTube videos to your WooCommerce product pages with ease using Product Page YouTube for WooCommerce. 

This simple, yet powerful, free plugin allows you to integrate videos from YouTube directly on your product pages, giving your customers a richer shopping experience.

See more in documentation via [this website](https://shieldclimb.com/free-woocommerce-plugins/product-page-youtube/).

== Installation ==

1. Download our plugin from https://shieldclimb.com/free-woocommerce-plugins/product-page-youtube/
2. At your WordPress admin, Go to Plugins > Add New Plugin > Upload Plugin > Choose File (Choose the file you downloaded just now) > Click Install
3. Click Activate
4. By default, the YouTube video will autoplay, be muted, and loop automatically to ensure smooth user experience. The video will remain muted to avoid sudden sound interruptions, which could startle visitors. However, viewers have full control to unmute, adjust volume, rewind, or pause the video as needed.
5. Once your video is added and settings configured, save your changes, and the YouTube video will appear on your product page for customers to interact with!

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Can I add multiple videos to a single product page? =

The plugin is designed for one video per product page

= Does this plugin work with all WooCommerce themes? =

Yes, the plugin is compatible with most WooCommerce themes. If you use the Kadence theme, a workaround may be required by removing extra product gallery images.

= Will this plugin slow down my website? =

The Product Page YouTube for WooCommerce plugin is lightweight and optimized, but autoplay videos may slightly impact page load times, especially on mobile or if the video is above the fold. We recommend testing the page load speed to ensure optimal performance for your site.

= Will the YouTube video autoplay on product pages? =

Yes, the video autoplays by default, but it’s muted and set to loop. Customers can unmute, pause, or rewind the video as they wish.

= Is the plugin mobile responsive? =

Yes, the embedded YouTube video is fully responsive and works smoothly on all devices, including mobile and tablets.
